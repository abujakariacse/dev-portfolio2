Zunayed Evan's World

Musical Artist & Novelist

Zunayed Evan is a Bangladeshi musical artist, composer, author and internet personality who is the owner of Studio Apollo Z, a Music Production Studio in Bangladesh. He is also a band member of Ashes.

Story of Success

Become a Musician & Writer

Zunayed Evan started music carrier for “The 69 – Band Mixed Album” in 2010 which was Organized By: Radio 2 Fun and music Label was Fahim Music. in 2012, he worked in “Hatiar – Band Mixed Album” which was Organized By: Isha Khan Duray and music Label G series. In 2014, he started working with “ASHES” and Charpoka was debut album by ASHES. In 2016, he worked in “ROCKoholic – Band Mixed Album” with aajob record label.
